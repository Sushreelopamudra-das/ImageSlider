ODISHA
INDIA'S BEST KEPT SECRET
NATURE
The state of Odisha is home to some of nature's finest wonders. On one hand you have the mesmerising Chilika Lake, on the other there are the beaches. Odisha is also home to some dramatic waterfalls that are a must-visit. At Similipal National Park you will get to see the state's widely known wildlife, while the Olive Ridley beach is yet another example of its diverse wildlife.
